AI YouTube Factory – Full Stack Autonomous Content Creation Platform
Build a production-ready, enterprise-grade, GitHub-ready AI web application that automatically creates, edits, renders, manages, and uploads storytelling videos from only Topic and Summary provided by the user.

The system should require no manual intervention after the initial input.

The entire project must follow Clean Architecture, SOLID principles, Domain Driven Design where appropriate, modular design, plugin architecture, extensive comments, production logging, Docker deployment, and professional software engineering best practices.
